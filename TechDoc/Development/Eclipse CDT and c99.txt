Setup "c99" mode for Eclipse CDT
2010.06.30 - 2012.02.13


  For Eclipse and CDT,
    open "Project" / "Properties" from toolbar,
    under tree "C/C++ Build" / "Settings",
    in tab "Tool Settings",
    under tree "GCC C Compiler" / "Miscellaneous",
    add "-std=c99" in "Other flags" field in left panel.
